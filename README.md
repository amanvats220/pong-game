
## 🎨 Game Elements

- **Green Paddle**: Your paddle (left side) - controllable
- **Red Paddle**: Computer paddle (right side) - AI controlled
- **Yellow Ball**: The game ball with physics
- **White Center Line**: Visual center divider
- **Scoreboard**: Real-time score display

## 🤖 AI Difficulty

The computer AI uses intelligent ball tracking with a slight delay to create a challenging but fair opponent. The AI:
- Tracks the ball's position
- Predicts ball movement
- Adjusts paddle position smoothly
- Stays within game boundaries

## 🛠️ Technical Details

- **Language**: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6)
- **Canvas API**: Used for game rendering
- **Animation**: RequestAnimationFrame for smooth 60 FPS gameplay
- **No Dependencies**: Pure vanilla JavaScript, no frameworks required

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

1. **Aim for Spin**: Hit the ball at the edges of your paddle to add spin
2. **Predict Shots**: Watch the AI's movement to anticipate ball direction
3. **Control Speed**: The ball speeds up with each paddle hit - keep it in control
4. **Use the Sides**: Hitting the ball near the paddle edges creates stronger angles
5. **Stay Centered**: Keep your paddle near the center for better reaction time
